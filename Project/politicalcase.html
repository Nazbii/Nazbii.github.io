Watergate Scandal

The Watergate Scandal was a major political scandal in the United States in the 1970s. It stemmed from the Nixon administration's attempts to cover up its involvement in the break-in at the Democratic National Committee headquarters at the Watergate office complex.

The scandal ultimately led to the resignation of President Richard Nixon, the first and only time a U.S. president has resigned from office.
